Project Idea

Overview

Campus Search APP is a third-party platform (IOS platform, Android platform), for college students, aggregating information around the university, food, clothing, housing and transportation, classification and centralized release, convenient for college students to study and live application software. It is the same as Yiyi.com, Dazhongdianping and other life service APP software in Australia: both are to serve consumers, both provide information for consumers, convenient for consumers to query. What is different from other service APPs is that Campus Search APP targets college students and subdivides the consumers, while all the contents released by APP are combined with students' life experience, and then released after being collected and screened by special data collectors.

Motivation

As world's Internet user base grows larger and larger, the Internet industry is playing an important role in the market economy. At the same time, the advent of the information age has completely changed people's traditional means of communication. From sending letters to making phone calls, from making phone calls to talking online phones, from talking cell phones to smart phones that can access large amounts of information, the main means of people's access to information has undergone a qualitative change. People are not only satisfied with the function of talking and texting, but also use cell phones as a tool for receiving information, understanding current affairs, establishing social networks, facilitating life and playing games. The acceptance and reliance of college students on various information platforms are generally very strong, but there are a lot of mixed applications of life services on the market, and there are few applications that provide information to facilitate the study life of college students with the basic starting point of the interests of college students. So the campus search APP for college students is born.

Description

Campus APP is a smart phone application for students in specific schools, which aims to provide localized learning and living information and certain social functions for students, its functions include: campus information, information query, living services, local dating and other aspects, which can not only provide various convenient services for college students in life, learning and entertainment, but also provide a good opportunity for our college students to start their own business. It also provides a good opportunity for students to start their own business.

The services of APP provide:

1. map and navigation around the campus: marking the location around the school (such as cafes, gyms), while supporting the sharing of locations between users.

2. Store reviews in and around campus: star ratings for each store in the surrounding area, initial ratings with reference to other review APPs, continuous updates from users, support for store collections and sharing.

3. Class schedule and classroom guide function: the APP includes the time and classroom location of the class, and also provides a detailed classroom location plan, with the classroom highlighted. Provide the function of alarm clock before class, which can be set independently to remind how long before class.

4. Campus information push function: real-time information on each campus network, classification of information and setting of tags, users can pay attention to each tag, the information of the tags that users pay attention to will be pushed in real time (including all kinds of notices, results release time, news, lecture information, etc.).

5. Life information push function: pushing information on merchants' special offers, and constantly updated product information such as cinemas, etc. in a similar tagging manner.

APP part is divided into two versions for ios system and Android system, the interface design is guided by a simple and clear style, divided into three parts: user, function and software feedback.

User part is the part where users manage their accounts and personal information, supporting default (i.e. visitor login), including information management of accounts, management of tag groups, management of custom tags, setting of push information, saving of class schedule information, and management of collection information.

Function part is the part of software function implementation, including the association with Google Maps to provide navigation, the association with software such as Instagram to provide sharing of information such as addresses or stores, the association with alarm clocks for reminders, the information push system, and the system for networking to obtain store information, and the system for implementing user and software feedback. Software feedback is the part where the software is set up and where the user contacts the software designer. It includes the basic settings of the software (login and logout, whether to push messages, version updates, etc.), feedback (submitting comments and ideas in text form), and business contact (campus activities that need to be promoted are contacted through this channel with a dedicated person).

There is also a corresponding APP background management system, which is responsible for managing the uploaded store information and various timely updated push messages. At the same time, it records various information such as users' search records for future upgrades and improvements.

Tools and technologies

Use JAVA to create Android version, and use the iOS-specific SDK released by Apple to create the iOS version. As the APP market is full of pirated software, APP users are very likely to download pirated software unintentionally and suffer losses from pirated APPs in the process of using them, at which time, uninformed APP users may wrongly point the finger at the operator or developer of the genuine APP.

The ways to deal with this risk are usually:

1. Prompt users to download the APP through official channels in a prominent position on the APP developer's website, and avoid downloading through unofficial channels such as third-party APP markets and forums.

2. You can add a program in APP to prompt users to verify whether it is a genuine APP when it is launched for the first time.

3. Provide users with a way to verify the genuine APP through technical means (such as MD5 code verification, etc.).

4. Use in the website user agreement and APP user agreement for users.

Skills required

1. APP UI: The designer carries out the design of the prototype according to the requirements, including the structural distribution of functions, the design of the sub-pages and the design of the business logic between pages.

2.Server side: writing the interface protocol documentation, setting up the server environment (Amazon), designing the database and writing the API interface.

3.APP side: developing interface according to UI design drawings, entering and interfacing with the server-side interface, obtaining data through the server-side interface and writing functional logic code.

4.Web management side: according to the front-end business logic, the back-end will have the corresponding function to match it, need to write the logic code on the function.

5.Testing and debugging: After the app function is developed, the testers will systematically test the whole project.
